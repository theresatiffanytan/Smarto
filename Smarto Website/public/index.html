About Us

Smarto is an application that your guests to book and check-in to your hotel. From mobile app, your guests will be able to book their selected room and choose the date for their booking. Smarto app will also help them to check in via mobile app so it will be easier for them to access your service.

In the other hand, Smarto website will help you to manage your hotel's rooms. You can see which of your rooms that being occupied and available. You can also see the most likeable room from your hotel to help you set the price for each room that available.
